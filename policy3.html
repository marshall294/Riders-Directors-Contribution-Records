Riders Football Club

Meeting Schedules & Guidelines

Overview

Meetings are an essential part of Riders Football Club’s administration, ensuring proper coordination, planning, and accountability. All meetings are held in accordance with the Constitution of the Club and are aimed at fostering unity and smooth operations.

Monthly General Meeting

Every Last Saturday of the Month

The official Riders FC Monthly General Meeting holds on the last Saturday of every month. This meeting focuses on reviewing club activities, planning upcoming programs, addressing member concerns, and strengthening the club’s internal coordination.

Quorum: At least two-thirds (2/3) of members must be present.

Emergency Meetings

As the Need Arises

Emergency meetings may be convened by the Executive Committee or Board of Trustees when urgent issues arise that require immediate attention. Notification will be sent to members promptly through official communication channels.

Types of Meetings (as stated in the Constitution)

1. Annual General Meeting (AGM)

Held once a year, this meeting reviews the club’s annual performance, financial reports, and future plans. Quorum required: 2/3 of members.

2. Special General Meeting

Convened to address special or urgent matters affecting the club. Quorum required: 2/3 of members.

3. Meeting of the Executive Board

Dedicated to strategic planning and high-level decision-making. Minimum quorum: 2 members of the Board of Trustees.
