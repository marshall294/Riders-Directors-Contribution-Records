Riders Football Club

Board of Trustees

Introduction

The Trustees of Riders Football Club are appointed in accordance with the provisions of the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) regulations and the Club’s Constitution. They serve as custodians of the club’s legal, structural, and governance framework.

Appointment of Trustees

Selection Process

Trustees are elected during a General Meeting with a 2/3 majority vote of members present. The number of trustees must be not less than 2 and not more than 15, as stipulated by law.

Tenure of Office

A Trustee may hold office for five (5) years but shall cease to hold office if any of the following occur:

  • Resignation from office
  • Loss of membership of the Registered Trustees
  • Becoming mentally incapacitated
  • Declaring bankruptcy
  • Conviction for a dishonesty-related criminal offence
  • Recommended removal via majority vote at a General Meeting
  • Relocation outside Nigeria

Filling a Vacancy

When a vacancy occurs, a General Meeting shall be convened to appoint a new eligible member to serve as Trustee.

Common Seal

The Trustees shall maintain a Common Seal, kept securely by the Secretary. Documents requiring execution must be signed by authorized Trustees and sealed accordingly.
