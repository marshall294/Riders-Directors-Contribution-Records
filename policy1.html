Riders Football Club

Official Aims & Objectives

Our Guiding Purpose

Riders Football Club is built on values of passion, unity, excellence, integrity, and community service. These aims and objectives represent the foundation of our operations and the legacy we are committed to building both on and off the field.

Aims & Objectives

1. Inspire Passion & Foster Sportsmanship

To inspire a deep passion for football, foster strong sportsmanship, and empower individuals through teamwork and athletic excellence. Riders FC is dedicated to creating an inclusive and positive environment where players of all ages and backgrounds can grow, learn, and thrive.

2. Promote Collaboration & Unity

To believe in and champion the power of collaboration, unity, and collective effort. Through teamwork, we achieve more—on the field, within the club, and in our communities.

3. Encourage Respect & Fair Play

To respect the game of football, our opponents, coaches, teammates, officials, and supporters. Riders FC upholds the highest standards of sportsmanship, fairness, and ethical conduct.

4. Pursue Continuous Excellence

To strive for excellence in all aspects of the club—from player development, coaching, and management to competitions and community engagement. We consistently challenge ourselves to improve and be the best version of our club.

5. Promote Inclusivity & Diversity

To welcome players from all backgrounds and abilities, fostering a diverse football family where everyone has equal opportunities to succeed. Inclusion remains a core value we uphold.

6. Engage the Community

To actively engage with the local community, especially the youth, promoting sportsmanship, discipline, health, and overall well-being. Riders FC strives to be a positive influence beyond the pitch.

7. Build a Legacy of Talent & Impact

To become a leading football club known for nurturing young talent, sustaining a deep love for the game, and making a lasting positive impact on society. We aim to build a legacy of excellence that future generations will proudly inherit.
